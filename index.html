Our Heritage

A Vision Born in Faith & Courage

In the 1990s, Gavin and Rhoda Jordaan discovered a calling during their visits to Madagascar. They recognised a deep need for quality, faith-based education on this beautiful island. In 1999, they courageously relocated from South Africa, and in 2000, the seeds of Vision Valley School were planted in their own home in Androhibe.

From those humble beginnings — just three children — the school has grown into a flourishing community of over 250 students spanning Preschool through Grade 12. Today, our graduates attend universities in Canada, the United States, the United Kingdom, South Africa, and beyond.

The medium of instruction is English, complemented by French and conversational Malagasy — preparing every student for both local impact and global contribution.
